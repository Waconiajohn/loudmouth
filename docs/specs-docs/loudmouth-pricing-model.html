The Caching Advantage
Music.AI charges per processing minute — but you only process each unique song once and cache it. After your 500th user unlocks "Take Me to Church," every subsequent user gets it free. Your effective per-user cost on Music.AI drops to near-zero as your catalog matures. This is the strongest argument for Music.AI at scale.
🛡️
Why AudioShake SDK Wins Early
In month 1, every user is unlocking songs no one has processed yet. Your Music.AI cost could hit $1.40/user in the first months as you build catalog. AudioShake SDK revenue share protects you — you pay a flat per-user rate regardless of how many new songs are processed.
📱
On-Device Means Zero Storage Cost
The AudioShake SDK processes audio on the user's phone in real time. You never store any audio files on your servers. No storage costs. No bandwidth costs. No legal exposure from hosting processed audio. The user's phone does the work every time they sing.
🔄
The Optimal Strategy
Start with AudioShake SDK revenue share while catalog is small. Negotiate hard — anchor at $0.25, target $0.35. Build in volume discount triggers at 10K and 50K users. At 50K+ subscribers with a mature catalog, renegotiate to a flat annual fee — at that scale it's significantly cheaper.
