See Your Voice in Real Time
Most people don't know this: you cannot accurately hear your own voice while you're singing. Bone conduction makes it sound richer in your head than it does to the room. You've been training deaf your whole life. LOUDMOUTH's real-time pitch display shows your voice as a line on screen while you sing — objective, instant, and accurate.
