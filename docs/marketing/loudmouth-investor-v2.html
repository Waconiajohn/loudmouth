The Problem
Karaoke Ruins Parties.

Someone always ruins karaoke night. They don't know they can't sing — and there's no fix. Every existing app plays fake re-recordings, has no real coaching, and no mechanism to help bad singers sound good in the moment.

$6B market. Zero products that actually solve the core user problem.

The Solution
Borrow a Superstar's Voice.

LOUDMOUTH's Vocal Assist Slider lets users blend a real artist's voice with theirs — up to 90%. Sound incredible tonight. Then, with AI coaching backed by real vocal science, gradually reduce the assist until they're singing solo.

Tonight's fix. Tomorrow's training. One product.
